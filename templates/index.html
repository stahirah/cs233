VIRAL PATHOGENESIS

Viral Pathogenesis involves a series of specific events necessary for the virus to gain access into the target host cell, hijack cellular machineries, reproduction and assembly of replicated viral parts, and finally the exit of new viral copies. This project utilizes all experimental data about Sars-Cov2 virus to present the pathogenesis of covid.
