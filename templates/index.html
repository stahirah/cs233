ABOUT

Viral Pathogenesis involves a series of specific events to gain access into the target host cell, hijack cellular machineries for viral reproduction, assemble of replicated viral parts, and finally the exit of new viral copies. This project utilizes all experimental data about Sars-Cov2 virus to present the pathogenesis of covid.
